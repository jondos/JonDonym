CookieCuller

This Firefox plugin enables you to select (protect) wanted cookies and delete the rest. Deletion can be done manually or automatically during startup of Firefox. This means, that cookies will be accepted (which might be important for proper functioning) but deleted later if you didn't mark them as worth having. So "bad" sites will no longer be able to recognize you which means that they cannot generate a profile of you.
  • Installation

      These are two ways to go to the plugin's installation website:

    • Open this link to go to the installation website directly.

      OR

    • Choose Tools from the menu, then Extensions. A window showing all installed extensions will appear. Click Get more extensions to reach the plugin site for firefox. Perform a search for "CookieCuller".

    Click on "Install Now". Approve the installation and restart your browser.

  • Settings

    Open the window with all plugins installed as described above. Choose CookieCuller and click Settings.
    Activate checkbox Delete unprotected cookies on startup.

  • Selection

    Now open CookieCuller in the menu Tools -> CookieCuller. Select all cookies that you want to keep for sure and press Protect Cookie. You may now delete all other cookies by pressing Remove all Cookies. Your protected cookies will be kept.
