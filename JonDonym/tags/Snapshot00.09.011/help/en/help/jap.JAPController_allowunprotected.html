Surfing without anonymity mode

JonDo prevents you from unintentionally surfing without being connected to any anonymiser service. Although, you have the possibility to allow this explicitly.

By default, JonDo will ask you again for a choice after a few seconds. If you choose to remember your answer, it will be saved and JonDo won't ask you again. If you have decided to allow non-anonymous surfing, JonDo will never remember your answer permanently, as this would be a potential security risk four you. Your answer is only remembered until you reconnect to any anonymity service or you restart JonDo.
