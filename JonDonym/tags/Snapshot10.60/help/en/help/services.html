Services

To get to this settings panel click Settings in JonDo's main window and then choose InfoService from the list to the left.

The settings available to you here concern the access to the anonymity services AN.ON and JonDonym. In the beta version there will also be an option to access the anonymizers Tor and Mixminion. These functions are experimental though and it is not guaranteed that access to these services works with JonDo.

  • AN.ON/JonDonym
    Contains all settings for access to the services AN.ON and JonDonym.

  • Tor
    Experimental access to the Tor network
    Beta version, only available in Expert View.

  • Mixminion
    Experimental access to the Mixminion-Remailer-Service.
    Beta version, only available in Expert View.

  • Advanced settings
    More general settings for the anonymization services.
    only available in Expert View.
