JonDo's features

JonDo - The Privacy Generator

The program JonDo (a branch of JAP) allows your browser to contact websites over an IP address within the JonDonym system. This means, that neither the contacted server, nor any eavesdropper on your connections, nor the operators of the JonDonym service themselves can realize which websites you surf. The premium services allow for an additional anonymization of the following applications:

  • IRC Chat Clients (e.g. Chatzilla, integrated in JonDoFox).
  • Messengers (e.g. Pidgin, recommend with the Jabber protocol and the OTR plugin).
  • And basically all programs for which you can configure a HTTP proxy.

Email clients and VoIP are currently not supported. To send anonymous emails we recommend you dispense with email clients and resort to web based mail services over the browser. Almost all email providers offer such webmail access.

What JonDo is

  • JonDo is an application that spoofs your own IP for web access by anonymized applications (e.g. by the web browser).
  • JonDo is an Anti-Censorship-Application that allows access to otherwise blocked Internet sites. "JonDonauts" may also configure their JonDo as a forwarding server in order to allow others access to the JonDonym service.
  • JonDo is open source and free.
  • JonDo filters and anonymizes HTTP headers.
  • JonDo is available on all major systems (Windows, Mac OS X, Linux).
  • JonDo allows to free and chargeable (premium) services.
  • JonDo was and is developed by the JonDos GmbH in cooperation with German universities (TU Dresden, Universität Regensburg) and privacy organizations (Unabhängiges Landeszentrum für Datenschutz Schleswig-Holstein).

What JonDo is not

  • JonDo does NOT make your PCs IP invisible.
  • JonDo is NOT a filesharing anonymization tool.
  • JonDo can NOT anonymize emails (except webmailers).
  • JonDo does NOT transparently integrate with your operating system, i.e. every applications has to be configured separately.
  • JonDo does NOT accelerate Internet access.
  • JonDo is NOT JonDonym (JonDonym is the offered service, JonDo the client used to access the service).
  • JonDo is NOT JonDos (JonDos is the company, that developed JonDo).
  • JonDo can NOT use any "anonymous" proxies (e.g. those on lists of open proxies) to create an anonymous connection. This works only with JonDonym mix cascades which are certified, automatically listed in JonDo and do not have to registered separately.
  • JonDo does NOT change the IP constantly but keeps the same IP as long as it is connected to the same mix cascade. This could be minutes, hours or even days.
  • JonDo is NO One-Click-Anonymization-Tool - something like that can and will not really exist ever! Prudence is and remains maxim for Internet security.
  • JonDo filters No plugins, scripts or HTTPS websites. To secure your browser as a whole you best use JonDoFox.
