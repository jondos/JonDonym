Anonymity

This group of settings can be accessed by clicking Settings in the main window. In the settings menu you will find the point Anonymity on the left side.

The following settings relate to fundamental functions of JonDo.

  • Services
    Here you can check information about the available services and change further settings.

  • InfoService
    InfoServices supply information about available services and updates to the application. Here you can administer JonDo's access to InfoServices.
    only in Expert View.

  • Forwarding server
    Contains all settings necessary for using your JonDo as a forwarding server. Users can then reach mix services through your JonDo.
    only in Expert View.

  • Certification authorities
    Certificates are necessary for ensuring authenticity of mixes, InfoServices and their operators. You can administer the root certificates of the program here.
    only in Expert View.

Please remember
JonDo contains all necessary settings preconfigured so that you can connect to the available services right away. Changing these settings is not needed usually.
