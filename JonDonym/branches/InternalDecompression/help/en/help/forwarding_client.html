Forwarding Client

To get to this panel, press button Settings in JonDo's main window, then choose the entry Forwarding Client from the list to the left.

Forwarding Client Settings

  • I need the help of a forwarder to contact the anonymity service:
    Select this checkbox if you can not reach the IP address of the anonymity service because that IP address is blocked by a firewall, by your Internet Service Provider or by your government.
    When this option is set, you can use the connections of other JonDo users to gain access to the anonymity service.

  • I'm also not able to reach an InfoService directly.
    Select this checkbox if the IP address of the InfoService is also blocked.
